About us

UJSARIO or Sahrawi Youth Union, is and organization made up from students and youth all over Western Sahara. We want to ensure we get to grow up to a real future, we want to go sto school and we want to be able to live our lives without worry, as kids should.

Western sahara is the last remaining colony in Africa, but we don't want to stay a colony. We want independence and freedom, we want rights, and we want education. Help us claim the freedom we never got, help us create a government that wants whats best for our country. We want to be given the same chance everyone else is given. Help us break of these shackles that is Morocco.

For years we have taken damage from Morokko and SADR 's feud. They both want to own us, but we will not be owned. Will you stand with us against the oppression? All you have to do is support us by signing up, we want to tell the world how many stand by our side. If you want to give support us further, you can help us build an education by donating.
